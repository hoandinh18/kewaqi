KEWAQI INTRODUCTION

TAIZHOU KEWAQI TOOLS CO.,LTD. was established in December 2014. Our company is located  in Luqiao pengjie Town industrial area，a coastal city -- Taizhou City, Zhejiang Province, with convenient transportation. We specialize in producing Garden machinery, agricultural machinery, small gasoline engine, lawn mower, brush cutter, hedge trimmer, digging machines and other products. The company owns a number of capable management team and production teams.

We always take the "manufacture of garden machinery product safety and environmental protection, to provide customers with green power, did the industry leader"for business purposes, insist on quality first, credibility, management-oriented, service as a business philosophy.
